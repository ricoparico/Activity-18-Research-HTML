Arthur Nery

Arthur Madrigalejos Nery (born January 28, 1997) is a Filipino singer and songwriter.
He signed a record deal in 2019 under Viva Records, and released his debut album Letters Never Sent in the same year.
He became well known in 2021 for his hit single "Pagsamo"
